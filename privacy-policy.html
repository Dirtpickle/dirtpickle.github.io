Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

Dirtpickle ("we," "us," or "our") operates mobile games available on Google Play. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our games.

1. Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our games, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game progress, session duration, features used, in-game actions
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and technical diagnostics
  • Advertising Data: Ad views, clicks, and interactions

Information Provided by Third Parties

We use third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases and account authentication
  • Advertising Networks: To deliver personalized ads based on your interests
  • Analytics Services: To understand how our games are used and improve user experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To provide core game features and save your progress
  • In-App Purchases: To process transactions and deliver purchased content
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads and measure ad performance
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve game design
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Third-Party Services

Our games integrate the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

Google Play Services

Used for in-app purchases and game services. View their privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Advertising Networks

We use third-party advertising networks to display ads in our games. These networks may collect and use data to show you personalized ads. Common advertising partners include:

  • Google AdMob: Privacy Policy
  • Other ad networks as disclosed in-game

Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to collect usage statistics and improve our games.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who help us operate our games (ad networks, analytics, payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

5. In-App Purchases

Our games offer in-app purchases processed through Google Play's billing system. We do not directly collect or store your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Google Play according to their privacy policy and terms of service.

6. Advertising and Personalization

We display advertisements in our games through third-party ad networks. These ads may be personalized based on your interests and activity.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Adjusting your device's advertising settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization)
  • Visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page: www.aboutads.info/choices

Note: Opting out does not mean you will see fewer ads, only that they will be less relevant to your interests.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. When information is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising (see Section 6)
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure that your data receives adequate protection wherever it is processed.

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Note: We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

15. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: When you provide explicit consent
  • Contract: To fulfill our contractual obligations (e.g., in-app purchases)
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our games and prevent fraud
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

This privacy policy is designed to comply with Google Play requirements and general privacy regulations. For specific legal advice, consult with a qualified attorney.
