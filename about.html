Creative journey and artistic process

I'm a multidisciplinary creative who builds complete projects across games, art, and web. I ship work—not just concepts.

Game Development

I've shipped a pixel art rogue-like built in Construct 3. Since then, I've been developing modular game systems in Godot, specializing in data-driven architecture: JSON dialogue trees, component-based NPCs, extensible quest systems. My approach: if adding a feature requires rewriting existing code, the system needs work.

Visual Work

Direction & Collaboration

Strong creative director with experience managing artists and maintaining cohesive visual style across projects. On my shipped game, I directed multiple artists to produce assets that fit the game's aesthetic.

Other Skills

Video editing, web development (like this site—designed and built by me), UI/UX design, and audio production. Background in tattooing and music composition informs how I approach visual storytelling and user experience.

Philosophy

I use AI as a force multiplier for technical execution, which lets me focus on creative problem-solving, system design, and visual storytelling. I'm the director and designer—AI is the assistant.
