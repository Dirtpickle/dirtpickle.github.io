Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

Dirtpickle ("we," "us," or "our") operates mobile games available on Google Play. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our games.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information directly.

However, our games use third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases and app distribution
  • Advertising Networks: To display ads in our games

2. Third-Party Data Collection

The following third-party services may collect data when you use our games:

Google Play Services

Handles in-app purchases and may collect:

  • Purchase history and transaction data
  • Device information
  • Google account information (if signed in)

Advertising Networks

Display ads and may collect:

  • Device information (device type, OS version, unique identifiers)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • Location data (approximate, for ad targeting)
  • Usage patterns for personalized advertising

We do not have access to or control over this data. It is collected and managed entirely by these third-party services.

3. Third-Party Privacy Policies

For detailed information about how these services handle your data, please review their privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob (Ads): https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not collect or share your personal information. All data collection is performed by the third-party services listed above, which operate under their own privacy policies.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely through Google Play's billing system. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Google Play.

6. Advertising

Our games display advertisements through third-party ad networks. These ads may be personalized based on your interests.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Adjusting your device's advertising settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization)
  • Visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page: www.aboutads.info/choices

Note: Opting out does not mean you will see fewer ads, only that they will be less relevant to your interests.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Third-party services integrated in our games have their own age restrictions and privacy protections.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, we do not have access to your information. For requests related to data collected by third-party services (Google Play, advertising networks), please contact those services directly using their privacy policies linked above.

You can control advertising preferences through your device settings (see Section 6).

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Additional Information

California Residents (CCPA)

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act.

European Residents (GDPR)

We do not collect or process personal data. Third-party services (Google Play, ad networks) act as independent data controllers. For GDPR-related requests, contact those services directly using the privacy policy links in Section 3.
